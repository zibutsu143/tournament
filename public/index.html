Tournament Rules Overview

Format & Scoring

WPA 8-Ball Rules apply. Winner break format. Group matches: Win = 3pts, Loss = 0pts. Tie-breaker is Head-to-Head result.

Match Length

Group Stage: Race to 5
Knockouts to SF: Race to 7
Final Match: Race to 9

Shot Clock

A strict shot clock will be introduced and utilized starting from the Semi-Final matches onwards.

Dress Code & Punctuality

Polo/shirt, tailored trousers & shoes required.
5 mins late = Forfeit 2 frames.
10 mins late = Match forfeited.
